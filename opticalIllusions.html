Optical Illusions

Description

"Optical Illusion" is a dynamic visual art work that uses p5.js to generate a particle system composed of multiple rectangles. Each particle creates an optical illusion by changing its position and color. The movement of particles is affected by keyboard input (up, down, left, and right arrow keys), allowing the user to change the direction of particle movement through these arrow keys. Additionally, the constant changing of the background and particles creates a flowing and pulsating effect that further enhances the optical illusion. The particle system uses Perlin noise to generate motion paths, making particle motion look more natural and smooth.

Reflection

This work demonstrates the power of p5.js in creating optical illusions and motion graphics. Through carefully designed animation and color changes, the work creates a visual experience that captures the viewer's gaze and arouses curiosity. The project is an exploration of motion graphics, user interaction and optical illusions, showcasing the fusion of creativity and technology in digital art.

Matthew's artwork takes a critical view of social, political, and cultural issues. Matthew references public issues, exploring the contradictions between humanity, nature, and science.
